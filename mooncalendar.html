🌙 Monthly Moon Calendar

🌙 About this Moon Calendar

This moon calendar is based on the natural lunar cycle, which lasts approximately 29.53 days. The Moon does not emit its own light; instead, we observe different phases depending on the portion of the Moon illuminated by the Sun as it orbits Earth.

🌌 Purpose

This system is designed to offer a symbolic and visual representation of the lunar cycle, combining astronomical accuracy with a clean and accessible design. It is ideal for visualization, reflection, and creative or thematic projects inspired by lunar energy.
